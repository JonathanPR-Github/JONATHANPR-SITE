One of my passions - Raskeladden and Dovreslubben

Raskeladden and Dovreslubben is an auto-scroller mobile game made by Funksoft between November 2023 and March 2024.

The game is about Dovreslubben having stolen the ski products from Funksoft's main company, and the main character, Raskeladden, aims to reclaim his skis. The game runs automatically, and the main objective is to avoid traps on the slopes.
